    The dataset used is taken from Data.gov.sg's Real-time Carpark Availability API. Their API is updated every minute, and so are the timings and availabilities reflected in this website. Any inaccuracies found in the data are the responsibility of Data.gov.

    Unfortunately, the API's data is limited to HDB Carparks only. However, this application will be expanded in the future to include Mall and URA Carparks too. Keep a look out for that!

    I built this website to help drivers in Singapore quickly identify if a carpark is full and to allow them to find alternative options nearby.

    Also, this website was built as part of a Mini Project requirement for my Traineeship with VISA.

    Welcome to my carpark availability application! My aim is to streamline your parking experience by providing you with information on available parking spaces.

    Key Features:
    Real-Time Updates: Finding parking spaces can be a hassle. This application provides you with real-time data on HDB carpark availability.

    Favourites: Tailor your experience by saving your most frequented carparks, giving you easy access to your favourite lots.

    Convenient Navigation: Going to a new carpark and you're unfamiliar with the neighbourhood? Our application conveniently links you to Google Maps to help find your way there!

    Hello, I am Khairul, the creator of this carpark application! I find inspiration in simple every-day solutions that we might take for granted. This is my first of many attempts at creating simple solutions for people. I hope you find it useful!
